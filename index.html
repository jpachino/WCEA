ABOUT ME

With love, I have authored two books. Together they tell much of my life story.

C5: From Impact to Recovery






C5 is my husband's story. He was riding his bicycle a short distance from our home in Baltimore, MD. As he was reaching his stride, a black sedan appeared on the opposite side of the two lane residential street. The driver, not wishing to wait the second or two for Mel to pass, decided to turn left. He cut Mel off. With no time to stop or manuever, Mel hit the car. The impact sent him flying into the sky and then crashing face first into the road. C5 is Mel's story. The story of an indomitable spirit, beyond exceptional medical care , and unparalleled community support.

A Question of Heritage






A Question of Heritage is a story of adoption. In this introspective memoir written as fiction, I recount my quest to find my biological family and roots. This story resonates with the angst of those in the adoption triad, yet leaves the reader with insight and hope.
